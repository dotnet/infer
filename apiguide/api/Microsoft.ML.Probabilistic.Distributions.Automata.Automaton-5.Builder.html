    Append(Automaton<TSequence, TElement, TElementDistribution, TSequenceManipulator, TThis>, Int32, Boolean)

    Creates an automaton f'(s) = sum_{tu=s} f(t)g(u), where f(t) is the current automaton (in builder) and g(u) is the given automaton. The resulting automaton is also known as the Cauchy product of two automata.
